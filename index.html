Why is this here?

This is just a DnD dice roller I made because I wanted to do like the rest of one of my DnD groups and make something related to DnD. I'm not much of an artisan, so I figured I'd write some code.


How do I use this site?

If you're reading this because you actually need help, then I failed because it was supposed to be fairly intuitive.

You select dice by clicking/tapping them at the top of the page. The selected dice show up in a box below. You click roll when you've selected what dice you want to roll and at what quantity.
If you want to remove a die from your selected list, you just click/tap it, and it will be removed.
